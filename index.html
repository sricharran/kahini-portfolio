About

I'm a psychologist with over three years of experience, working with individuals to understand their thoughts, emotions, and patterns in a way that feels clear and manageable.

My work is centred around creating a space that feels calm, open, and easy to step into—where you don't need to have everything figured out before you begin.

For Couples

Two people.

As a couple therapy practitioner, I work closely with couples, creating a space where both people feel heard, understood, and comfortable to express themselves.
